## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Tarikul-Islam-Anik/Animated-Fluent-Emojis/master/Emojis/People/Technologist.png" width="28" /> &nbsp;About Me

I'm an experimental physicist pursuing my **MPhil in Physics** at **Quaid-e-Azam University, Islamabad**. My research focuses on **memristor synthesis** and **neuromorphic devices** using polymers and ceramic materials, fabricating thin films via the sol-gel method.

When I'm not in the lab, I build high-performance desktop applications with **Rust** and **Tauri** -- creating tools that bridge the gap between science and software. I believe physics and code are two sides of the same coin.

> *From **Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan** -- where the mountains meet the sky.*
